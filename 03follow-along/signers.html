Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and later the third President of the United States.
John Adams
Strong advocate for independence, later became the second President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Leading intellectual and diplomat, played a key role in gaining support for independence.
Samuel Adams
Key leader in organizing resistance to British rule, known for his role in the American Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his bold and large signature on the Declaration of Independence.
Richard Henry Lee
Proposed the resolution for independence, a major political figure from Virginia.
